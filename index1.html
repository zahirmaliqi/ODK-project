Mrizi i Zanave Agroturisem

Located in Lezhë, Mrizi i Zanave Agroturizëm features a restaurant, garden and free WiFi throughout the property. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a children's playground. There is free private parking and the property provides paid airport shuttle service.

At the guest house, rooms have a patio with a garden view. At Mrizi i Zanave Agroturizëm each room has air conditioning and a private bathroom. Guests at the accommodations can enjoy a continental breakfast.

Location

Velipojë is 14 mi from Mrizi i Zanave Agroturizëm, while Shëngjin is 7.5 mi away. The nearest airport is Tirana International Airport Mother Teresa Airport, 33 mi from the guest house. Couples found the location very good, rated 9.4
